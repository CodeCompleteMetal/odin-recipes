Fig & Blue Cheese Burgers

Trader Joe’s Angus Chuck, Brisket, & Sirloin 1/3 lb. Ground Beef Patties boast a uniquely flavorful blend of three different cuts of well-marbled Angus Beef. The Brisket and Sirloin each contribute amazingly rich flavor, with their slightly fattier composition, while the ground Chuck brings additional ‘beefiness’ and balance. All that to say, these Patties make a seriously special burger.


So, we’re adorning these burgers accordingly with an elevated array of toppings: sweet TJ’s Fig Butter, savory TJ’s Triple Cream Soft Ripened Cambozola® Blue Cheese, umami-rich caramelized red onions, bright and peppery arugula—all atop a buttery TJ’s Brioche Bun.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons of your favorite TJ’s Olive Oil
  • 1 TJ’s Red Onion, thinly sliced
  • TJ’s Sea Salt
  • 1 package TJ’s Angus Chuck, Brisket, & Sirloin 1/3 lb. Ground Beef Patties
  • 1 wedge TJ’s Triple Cream Soft Ripened Cambozola® Blue Cheese, sliced into rectangles
  • 4 TJ’s Brioche Buns
  • TJ’s Fig Butter
  • TJ’s Arugula

Directions

  1. PrePare the Carmelized Onions

    Add 3 tablespoons of oil in a large non-stick skillet over medium-low heat. Add onion, then cover with lid and cook for 25-30 minutes, stirring frequently, until the onions soften and darken in color. Remove from heat and season with 1/2 teaspoon of salt. Set aside.

  2. Cook Burgers

    Heat a grill, large nonstick skillet, or grill pan to medium-high. Season burgers with salt, and cook, flipping at least once, until you reach your desired internal temperature. Place two slices of Cambozola® on top of each burger, then remove from heat, and let rest 5 minutes, allowing the cheese to gently melt from the residual heat.

  3. Toast Buns

    While the burgers rest, place buns cut-side down on the grill or pan. Toast for 2-3 minutes, until golden.

  4. Assemble

    Slather each bottom bun with fig jam, then add a handful of arugula. Place the burger on top, then a dollop of fig butter atop the cheese, followed by a spoonful of caramelized red onions. Top with bun to serve.
